Javascript: The Web's Flashy Bling Bling

Javascript is comparable to your average wrist watch. Just like your average wristwatch Javascript software is a marvel of engineering that uses hundreds of little pieces working in unison to accomplish a few major tasks that none of them could have without working alongside one another.

Javascript has made use out of the basic principles of encapsulation, abstraction, inheritance, and plymorphism to create some of the most widely adopted, groundbreaking, and user friendly technologies that we've come to know and love in our lifetime.

Javascript

Just like the wristwatch javascript has managed to stay relevant through many decades and has evolved alongside the world wide web. The use of Javascript has transcended time just like how the average wrist watch has evolved from the Vacheron Constantin to the Apple watch. In conclusion there are an infinite number of complications underneat the hood that allow the finish product to not only do the job but to do the job well in both wrist watches and Javascript (HTML's Hot New Bling).
