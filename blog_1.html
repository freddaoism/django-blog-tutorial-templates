CSS: The HTML's New Clothes

Technology 2021 April 12th Fred Dao 4 comments 588 views

CSS Personified

HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) is the naked ape that can communicate via smoke signals, pictures, text, and video.

CSS is the digital starched shirt, neck-tie, and dress pants that dresses the ape (HTML) up in order for it to be viewed as a functioning and well put together part of the web ecosystem rather than having it walk around with its assets hanging out for everyone to see.

CSS

Every beautiful website that you've ever surfed through uses CSS (Cascading Style Sheet) to tell the world about its personality the same way that you would buy a Versace bathrobe would tell the world that you had an extra 595 + shipping laying around. Just like with clothing there are a ton of online catalogues/guides that will give you premade "clothing" such as W3Schools, YouTube, and Stack Overflow.
