About Me

My name is Fred Dao and I am from San Jose, California. I grew up in Clover, South Carolina, went to school at the University of South Carolina and graduated with a BA in Communications in 2019. This purpose of this site is to track my progress in my web development journey and share my knowledge base as I continue to learn and grow in this field.

I am looking forward to leading teams, providing users with a valuable experience and making a lasting change on the technology sector.
